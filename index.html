Synopsis

Anne with an E is a Canadian television series adapted from the book Anne of Green Gables. The series narrate about important topics of society, such as orphaning, child abandonment, psychological trauma, social issues such as conformity, gender inequality, racism, religion, homosexuality, bullying and freedom of speech.
